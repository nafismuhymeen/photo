About

Gaston Salo

Gaston Salo is an FIU University student who spends his time watching and collecting photos of Miami's urban scene. Gaston enjoys "benching", a term used to describe watching trains go by to capture the graffiti written on them. Here this portfolio serves as a gallery to some of his works.

Hope you enjoy his works! If you have any question, or want to collaborate on some creative project, please don't hesitate to contact.

Have a nice day!
